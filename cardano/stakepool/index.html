How to Stake ADA

In order to stake ADA you will need to:

  1. Own ADA: Most people buy ADA through exchanges like Binance or Coindesk. You can also get ADA by changing other cryptos from Exodus wallet.
  2. Install one of the official Cardano wallets: Daedalus or Yoroi (Desktop, Iphone, Android). We recommend Yoroi because it's a light wallet, easy to setup and multi-platform.
  3. Create a wallet:Follow the instructions on your wallet software to create a wallet, be sure to store the recovery phrase/words in a safe place, best practice it’s to write it down by hand, never save it on a place that can be accessed by an attacker who finds out your password, since with these words all your ADA can be stolen.
  4. Fund your wallet: Now you can send your ADA from the exchange you bought them to your new wallet. Copy the "receiving address" exactly as shown in Yoroi/Daedalus and register a Cardano withdrawal address in your exchange account.
  5. Stake your ADA: Once you've received your ADA, you can create the delegation to start staking ADA, chose the steps below according to your wallet:

How to delegate ADA in Yoroi

Open your wallet and click “Delegation List” (top tabs)

Type in the ticker BZONE then click Search. Check that the ID matches our pool.

On the results you should find our pool BZONE. Click the ‘Delegate’ button.

You will be asked for your spending password.

Enter your password and confirm the delegation. Congratulations! you’re now staking with BZONE pool.

How to delegate ADA in Daedalus

Open your wallet and click on the "Delegation Centre" icon

On the rigth side of the wallet you wish to delegate, click on Delegate, then click on "Continue" to confirm.

Type in the ticker BZONE then click Search. Check that the ID matches our pool.

On the results you should find our pool BZONE. Select it and click ‘Continue’.

You will be asked for your spending password. Enter your password and confirm the delegation. Congratulations! you’re now staking with BZONE pool.
